Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 26, 2026

1. Overview

Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile and web application.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information from Google Sign-In

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture
  • Google Account ID

B. Financial Data You Enter

  • Transaction details (date, amount, category, description)
  • Transaction type (income, expense, loan)
  • Categories you create

C. Usage Data

  • App features you use
  • Time spent in app
  • Device type and operating system
  • General location (country/region)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Store your transactions securely
  • Display your financial data back to you
  • Sync transactions to your Google Sheet (if you choose)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Primary Storage: Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Backup Storage: Your personal Google Sheets (if you create them)
  • Security: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS

What We Control

  • We have access to your data in our systems
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Data is stored in secure Google data centers

What You Control

  • Your Google Sheet is entirely under your control
  • You can revoke sheet permissions anytime
  • You can delete your account and all associated data

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Export: Export your transaction data
  • Revoke: Stop the app from accessing your Google account anytime
  • Control: Manage which spreadsheets the app can access

7. Data Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • All transactions are permanently deleted from our database
  • Your profile is deleted
  • After 30 days, deleted data is purged from backups
  • Google Sheets you created remain under your control

To delete your account, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.

Your continued use of the App after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
